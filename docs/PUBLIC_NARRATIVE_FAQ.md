# Shekyl Public Narrative and FAQ

## Positioning Statement

Shekyl is a privacy-preserving digital cash network designed for real users, long-term resilience, and economic clarity.  
It combines battle-tested CryptoNote privacy foundations with a next-generation, self-regulating economic model:

- fixed total supply with practical denomination design,
- transaction-responsive release timing,
- adaptive fee burn,
- and lock-based participant alignment.

The goal is simple: make private payments safe and usable without sacrificing network security over time.

## One-Page Narrative

### Why Shekyl exists

Most digital assets force a trade-off between privacy, usability, and long-term security incentives. Shekyl is built to close that gap.

### What is technically different

Shekyl’s monetary system is designed as a self-regulating loop among miners, stakers, and transactors:

1. **Fixed supply, elastic release timeline**
  - Total supply is fixed.
  - Emission timing adapts to real network activity within bounded protocol limits.
2. **Adaptive fee burn**
  - A dynamic fraction of transaction fees is burned based on chain conditions.
  - This supports an automatic transition from growth to maturity dynamics.
3. **Lock-based participant alignment**
  - Participants who lock coins can earn from the protocol’s fee-flow mechanics.
  - Aggregate lock behavior acts as an implicit confidence signal.
4. **Perpetual security continuity**
  - A bounded non-zero terminal subsidy avoids a brittle fee-only end state.
  - This preserves stable security incentives through multiple market cycles.

### Why this matters socially

Privacy tech is dual-use, like encryption and cash. The relevant question is not whether misuse can exist, but whether the system is designed and communicated responsibly.

Shekyl’s stance is clear:

- protect lawful users,
- avoid predatory data exposure,
- maintain transparent protocol economics,
- and support responsible ecosystem participation.

## FAQ (Public-Facing)

### Why are Shekyl transactions private?

Shekyl is for anyone who values financial safety and confidentiality. Privacy protects ordinary users, companies, journalists, activists, and families from profiling, leakage, and coercion. Like encryption, privacy tools can be misused, but that does not define their legitimate purpose.

### Is Shekyl based on Monero technology?

Shekyl builds on proven CryptoNote/Monero lineage where appropriate because those components are battle-tested in production. We treat that as an engineering advantage, then extend the economic layer with Shekyl-specific mechanisms.

### Should we publicly mention Monero lineage?

Yes, with disciplined framing:

- emphasize reliability, maturity, and security track record,
- avoid sensational language,
- focus on legitimate user protections and economic design.

### How is Shekyl different from "copy-paste privacy coins"?

Shekyl combines privacy foundations with a coordinated economic architecture:

- bounded transaction-responsive emission timing,
- adaptive burn dynamics,
- lock-based participant incentives,
- and wallet-level legibility of chain health.

This is intended to improve sustainability and user comprehension, not just copy features.

### Is Shekyl inflationary or deflationary?

Both dynamics can exist at different phases. The system is designed to adapt with usage and maturity:

- early phases prioritize network bootstrapping and security continuity,
- mature phases can trend more deflationary through adaptive burn behavior.

The protocol publishes deterministic rules; outcomes are observable on-chain.

### Why not a pure fee-only model?

Fee-only end states are vulnerable to incentive instability in known mechanism-design literature. Shekyl keeps a bounded terminal subsidy to maintain predictable security incentives while allowing fee and burn mechanics to shape long-run net issuance.

### Does staking create governance politics?

Shekyl minimizes explicit governance overhead by using lock behavior as an implicit protocol signal. The intent is fewer political attack surfaces and more automatic economic adaptation.

### What is Shekyl’s compliance posture?

Shekyl is privacy-first and responsibility-aware:

- we do not market illicit use,
- we support lawful ecosystem participation,
- we favor clear documentation and transparent protocol rules,
- and we encourage integrators to apply jurisdiction-appropriate controls at service layers.

### What should exchanges, partners, and institutions know?

Shekyl prioritizes:

- mature privacy engineering,
- transparent monetary rule sets,
- explicit migration/versioning discipline,
- and long-horizon security economics.

It is built to be understandable, auditable, and stable under real-world usage.
